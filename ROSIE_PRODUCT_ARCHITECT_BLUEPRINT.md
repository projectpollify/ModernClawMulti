## What Rosie Proves Right Now
Rosie already proves that ModernClawMulti can:
- keep a baseline brain with a distinct product-development role
- give that brain a full architect-oriented knowledge layer
- separate internal product stewardship from external customer support
- maintain a distinct voice identity alongside Joe
- use live product truth as the basis for planning, quality review, and future rollout thinking

## What Rosie Does Not Mean Yet
Rosie does not mean the future architect workflow is fully productized.

Not built yet:
- wizard-driven product-planning flows
- dedicated architect entry points in the UI
- structured security review workflows
- rollout dashboards or formal release governance
- built-in role handoff between architect and support brains

So Rosie is currently:
- real
- useful
- validated
- but still a runtime-shaped architect brain, not a fully surfaced product-management feature layer

## Relationship To The Main Plan
Rosie should guide:
- core framework improvement
- product-quality decisions

- wizard system timing and scope
- future feature rollout sequencing

She gives us:
- a live architect persona to test against

- a concrete example of how internal expert brains should be seeded
- a strong counterbalance to support-only thinking

Rosie is the current live ModernClawMulti product architect brain.
