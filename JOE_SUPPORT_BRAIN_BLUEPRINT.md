# Joe Support Brain Blueprint

## Purpose
Joe is the current live ModernClawMulti support and customer service brain.

He exists to prove that the multi-brain system can support a real product-specialist agent, not just generic alternate personas.

## Current Role
Joe is intended to be:
- the main ModernClaw app tech support expert
- a customer service brain for confused or blocked users
- a calm product explainer who understands current app truth
- a bridge toward the future built-in support-brain feature layer

## Current Runtime Shape
Joe currently lives as a real runtime brain under:
- `%APPDATA%\LocalAI\agents\joe`

Important runtime files:
- `%APPDATA%\LocalAI\agents\joe\SOUL.md`
- `%APPDATA%\LocalAI\agents\joe\USER.md`
- `%APPDATA%\LocalAI\agents\joe\MEMORY.md`
- `%APPDATA%\LocalAI\agents\joe\knowledge\modernclaw-support-playbook.md`

## Seeded Brain Identity
Joe's current seed is built around these ideas:
- support first
- concise and reassuring
- exact about product truth
- strong at step-by-step troubleshooting
- honest about what is built versus planned
- beginner-friendly without sounding condescending

## What Joe Proves Right Now
Joe already proves that ModernClawMulti can:
- create a dedicated product-specialist brain
- give that brain its own isolated workspace
- give that brain its own support-specific files and knowledge pack
- give that brain its own voice identity separate from Rosie
- use the current app truth as the basis for product support

## What Joe Does Not Mean Yet
Joe does not mean the support-brain feature is fully productized.

Not built yet:
- dedicated UI entry points like `Get Help`
- built-in seeding through the app shell
- support-routing logic
- wizard integration
- formal support workflows for onboarding and escalation

So Joe is currently:
- real
- useful
- validated
- but still a runtime-seeded support brain, not a fully surfaced product feature

## Relationship To The Main Plan
Joe should inform the later support-brain and wizard phases.

He gives us:
- a live support persona to test against
- a concrete support voice and tone
- a real support knowledge pack structure
- a practical example of how customer-facing brains should be seeded

## Recommended Next Use
Joe should be used to test:
- product Q&A quality
- troubleshooting quality
- how well the refreshed docs support real customer help
- whether the support playbook produces the tone we want
- which support questions still expose gaps in the product truth pack

## Summary
Joe is the first real ModernClawMulti support brain.

He is not the final support-brain product feature yet, but he is the current live blueprint for it.
